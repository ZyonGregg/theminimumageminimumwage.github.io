Who We Are

At Minimum Age, Minimum Age, we advocate for the abolition of child labor as a youth-led movement to create a world where every child has the right to learn, rest, and play without condition.

Our Mission

We strive to elevate awareness about the endangerment of rights child labor has on younger generations, empower children to proper education and livelihood, and partner with organizations, schools, and policymakers to eradicate child labor as a whole and help the cause for a better future for these children.

“Let children be children.”

Why We Fight

Every child deserves a childhood—free from exploitation, filled with opportunity. Yet, millions are forced to sacrifice their future for survival.

We fight because child labor is not just a problem—it's a crisis of justice. It robs children of education, health, and hope. By raising our voices, we challenge the systems that enable it and build pathways to protection, education, and empowerment.

“We will not stop until every child is free to dream.”
